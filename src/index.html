one-stop-shop for salmon farm companies throughout Scotland

Our goal is for Ocean Farm Services to operate as a one-stop-shop for all Vessel Services required for any Salmon Farm in Scotland, covering their every need.

Ocean Farm Services Ltd. is a new company joining two well-established local Shetland firms, Whalsay based Aurora Marine Ltd. and Scalloway based North Isles Marine Ltd.

Both companies will combine forces to provide a stronger, broader Vessel service to Scotland’s growing Aquaculture industry.
