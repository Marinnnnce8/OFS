Aurora

  • Home
  • Our Vessels
  • Aurora

Aurora Marine’s workboat ‘Aurora’ was built in 2012 at the highly respected family owned Norwegian Shipyard ‘Viknaslipen’.

Aurora is of mono hull construction and is built from 8 and 6mm high grade marine aluminium. She is specifically designed and built for contract net cleaning, with a second engine room for housing the net cleaner power pack below deck. Keeping the equipment in better condition, free of salt water, whilst ensuring a clear deck to undertake other duties when net cleaning season is quiet.

Aurora has overall dimensions of 13.8m x 5m x 1.8m, powered by a V6 300HP Deutz main engine giving a top speed of 10.5 knots. There is also a 14KW 240V Lister generator fitted in the main engine room.
